We the People

We the People is a visualization that wants to show how many social media posts out of our dataset are related to a certain issue, and also within that subset, how many social media posts are related to a certain candidate as well.

How did we get this data? We used the Twitter and Facebook APIs to get comment and tweet data that is public. We have a pre-loaded dataset.

So, click on any outer bubble to examine an issue's division of social media posts. Clicking on any candidate within this outer bubble will take you to the Candidate-Issue view.

You can also filter the dataset by social media platform below.
